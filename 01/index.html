RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is a 3D interactive musical video to show what WebGL can do with the web. People can interactive with three different dreams. You can move the camera to every angle and make the animal to the place where your mouse point at and sometimes it will change the texture of the graphic. Sometimes there is a animal will run through the mouse where you point at. I think it created a new kind of experience of how web can provide. Now, the website is not only just the 2D picture and text. You can have a very different experience from what you had before. This video shows the potential of the future website can be. I wish to see more application of this kind of interactive video on the internet.

WebGL, Javascript, 3D visual, HTML
